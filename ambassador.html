Pure Detroit, a small business in the Motor City, reached out to fellow businesses to get them involved in their event.

2

On the day, participating businesses handed out passports to shoppers. Each time a shopper made a purchase, their passport was stamped.

3

Businesses thanked shoppers who had stamped passports with everything from discounts to cups of hot chocolate.

HOW THEY DID IT

Stay Local!, a local business organization in New Orleans, invited families to take part in their event.

3
